About Divij



I’m currently a freshman pursuing Computer Science and Engineering at University of California, Los Angeles, seeking internship opportunities in California. I’m passionate about creating efficient technological products for users — whether it be software, hardware or blends of both; I’m fond of competing at hackathons and creating innovative and worthwhile products. I undertake every task with zeal and utmost sincerity, and ensure quality output from my end.

I possess a secure skill set of C++, CSS, LabVIEW, HTML, as well as LaTex; I also have a fair understanding of Javascript and Excel. I am also interested in Design and Graphics, having a strong grasp of Photoshop, Illustrator and Gimp. Most importantly, I have developed an eagerness to learn anything new and my hunger for knowledge is never quenched.

When I’m away from computers, I love taking a retreat by pursuing my interests in debating and global politics. I’m not only an active member of UCLA’s Model UN Club, but also chaired as the Director of IAEA at BruinMUN 2016. I also take pleasure in doing community service and playing intra-mural basketball in college.

In 2016, I graduated from Delhi Public School, Gurgaon, honored with a Gold Medal for Continuous Academic Excellence. I was actively involved in the Student Council, being the Vice Head Boy and Founder & President of two community outreach organizations.
